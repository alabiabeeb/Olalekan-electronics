Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge electronic solutions that enhance lifestyles while maintaining the highest standards of quality, affordability, and customer satisfaction. We strive to make advanced technology accessible to every home and business in Nigeria.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted electronics retailer, recognized for innovation, reliability, and exceptional service. We envision a future where every Nigerian home enjoys the benefits of quality electronics that make life easier and more enjoyable.

Our Journey Since 2020

Founded in 2020, WIZZYMONEY Olamileken Electronics began as a small electronics store in Ibadan with a simple goal: to provide quality electronics at fair prices. What started as a passion project has grown into a trusted name in the electronics industry.

Over the years, we've expanded our product range from basic home appliances to include smart TVs, advanced sound systems, energy-efficient refrigerators, and innovative home automation solutions. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has helped us build lasting relationships with thousands of satisfied customers.

Today, we continue to evolve, bringing the latest technology to our customers while maintaining the personal touch and service excellence that defined our humble beginnings.
